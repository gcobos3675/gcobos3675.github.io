The Alarm Panel

The home security system starts with the ALARM PANEL.

The alarm panel is the heart of your security system. Without it, your doors and windows will never let you know if someone has opened them. This is where you can arm and disarm the system, adjust the volume of the chimes, and operate addition features such as home automation devices and security cameras if they are compatible.
