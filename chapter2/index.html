Home Glossary Lab 2 Classwork

Lesson 2 : Building a Webpage Template with HTML 5

Header (header, /header)

Header information of a webpage. Goes from Max (h1) to Min (h6).

Navigation (nav, /nav)

Contains hyperlinks to other webpages within a website and is located after the end "header" tag.

Main (main, /main)

Primary content of the webpage.

Footer (footer, /footer)

Places content of the very bottom of the webpage.

Section (section, /section)

Group of content of a webpage.

Article (article, /article)

Contains forum or blog posts.

Aside (aside, /aside)

Information about nearby content and is usually a sidebar.

Symbol Entities

&_copy; : Copyright Symbol

&_reg; : Registered Trademark

&_euro; : Euro

&_amp; : Ampersand

&_lt; : Less Than

&_gt; : Greater Than

&_nbsp; : Nonbreaking Space
