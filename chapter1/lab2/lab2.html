To plan a website, one must first understand the purpose of the website and know what the target audience for the website is, since it definitely will not be for everybody. Next, one must design the website to accommodate for varying types of devices that all vary from screen size, such as monitors, smartphones, and tablets. Then, one must design a flowing and well-made wireframe and site map to search through the website without feeling lost. Finally, one must find the right balance of graphics, color, and typography to made the website feel comfortable and easy on the eyes, as well as taking accessibility into consideration.
